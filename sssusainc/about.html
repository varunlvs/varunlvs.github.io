Founded in 2008 as a global logistics provider, Seven Seas Shipping USA, Inc. has provided logistics and freight management solutions for importers and exporters worldwide. As an NVOCC and Ocean Freight Consolidator, we are committed to delivering the highest level of customer service and support.

Utilizing our global strategic partners, we provide a wide range of logistics services ranging from domestic transportation to international air and sea freight services. Our mission is to create integrated supply chain solutions for our customers and enhance every business transaction with the appropriate value. We bring with us a network of international business intelligence and scientific management that aids analysis, design, discovery, planning, execution, and evaluations of the right logistics solution that steers you toward a strategic business advantage. Our customers enjoy not only customized services but also tailor-made evaluation criteria to guarantee strategic compliance to the business demands.
