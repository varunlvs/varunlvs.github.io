Seven Sea Shipping USA Inc

Founded in 2008, Seven Seas Shipping USA, Inc. is a global leader in providing logistics and freight management solutions for importers and exporters worldwide. As a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) and Ocean Freight Consolidator, we are committed to delivering the highest level of customer service and support.

Sea

Being a Global Forwarder, we provide flexible and strategic freight forwarding plans that are specific to the needs of each of our customers.

Government

We are a Licensed Customers Brokerage House and offer Customs Clearance Nationwide. Contact us to find out how we can help your business with all your inbound Customs clearances in the USA.

Air

As a TSA licensed corporation, we handle air freight around the globe, and have direct rate agreements with most major airlines.
